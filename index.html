Our Commitment

Built By Riders, For Riders.

FB Ontario started in a basement with a single mold and a passion for fingerboarding. We believe that true quality comes from attention to detail. Every deck is cured under pressure, hand-sanded, and finished to guarantee the perfect feel and pop right out of the box. We source the finest materials to ensure our gear can handle the gnarliest tricks you can throw at it.

  • 5-Ply North American Maple
  • Low-Friction Precision Bearings
  • Deep Concave and Responsive Kicks
